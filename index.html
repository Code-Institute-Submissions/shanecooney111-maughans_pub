MAUGHANS PUB

Who are we?

Maughans Pub is a family owned traditional but modern Irish pub located in Crossmolina, Mayo in the west of Ireland.

Open 7 days a week, drop in to enjoy a great pint of Guinness.

What we do

Pints! Here at Maughans we like to keep things simple and pints are simply what we do.

We offer a wide selection of draught beers, beer and wine by the bottle and a wide variety of spirits.

Live Sports? We got you covered!

Here at Maughans we offer many flat screen tvs located around the pub so you're sure to have a great view no matter where you sit!

We're sports fanatics, so wether its live GAA to Live Golf and everything inbetween, you're sure to catch it here at Maughans.
