MAUGHANS PUB

This isn't just any old yard!

What once was an area that housed farm animals for the old vetinary surgery attached to the pub now houses our brand new beer garden. Fitted with an 85 inch TV with surround sound, its a great place to watch a match on a sunny day. When the sun goes down we have various electric heaters situated around the yard for your comfort.

Events? We cater for all!

Want to throw a party? Our yard here at Maughans has become synonymous with hosting our famous BBQ's. Over the years we have raised tousands of euro for charity with numerous charity BBQ's.
Our yard is perfect for hosting any type of event from 18th birthday parties right upto 80th's with everything from wedding after parties, christenings and funerals inbetween!
