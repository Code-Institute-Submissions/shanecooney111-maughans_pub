This isn't just any old yard!

What once was an area that housed farm animals for the old vetinary surgery attached to the pub now houses our brand new beer garden. Fitted with an 85 inch TV with surround sound, its a great place to watch a match on a sunny day. When the sun goes down we have various electric heaters situated around the yard for your comfort.
